Spicy Lemon Rigatoni with Burrata

Prep Time: 15 Minutes Cook Time: 25 Minutes

Last weekend, I saw this excellent video of this Rigatoni pasta with Burrata (@delishaas/TikTok), so I decided to make it myself.

The recipe was relatively straightforward and took around 15 minutes to prep and 25 minutes to make.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound rigatoni pasta
  • 1 TBSP olive oil
  • 3 Shallots, thinly sliced
  • 2 TBSP red pepper flakes
  • 1/3 Cup fresh basil, chopped
  • 2 TSP dried oregano
  • 2 Lemons, juiced (can subsitite 1/3 cup lemon juice)
  • Zest from 1 lemon
  • 1 tube sun-dried tomato paste (2.8 oz)
  • 1 Cup heavy cream
  • 2 Cups mixed greens
  • 1/2 Cup shredded parmesan cheese
  • 8 oz burrata
  • parsley, red pepper flakes to taste

Directions

  1. Season 5 quarts of water with sea salt, bring to a boil, and then add the rigatoni. Cook to box instructions.
  2. Heat the olive oil in a large pan. Add the shallots, red pepper flakes, basil, oregano, lemon zest, and lemon juice; let cook for 1-2 minutes until shallots are golden brown.
  3. Add the sun-dried tomato paste to the pan and let cook down for 1-2 minutes.
  4. Add the heavy cream and let the sauce come to a simmer.
  5. Add the mixed greens and stir until they are cooked down.
  6. Add the parmesan cheese and season with salt and pepper to taste.
  7. When pasta is cooked, use a mug to save 1 cup of pasta water and strain.
  8. Toss pasta with the sauce, add the pasta water and let boil down.
  9. Top with parsley and generous amounts of burrata; serve immediately. Enjoy!
